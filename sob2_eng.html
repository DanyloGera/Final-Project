In the case of charity training (and in general with any activities in favor of the project), the win-win concept is the most honest. You get a quality service for which you would already pay money, but in this case, this money will also go to a good cause: we will buy products for the elderly, who now have a hard time. That is why we are often supported by friends, implementing such initiatives. On Saturday, Elena Kushnir and BK Alliance conducted a functional training with TRX elements for everyone and all this in the main arena of the country.

Training is a very special direction for us. After all, all our old men claim that they have lived to a ripe old age, including through sports or an active lifestyle. Therefore, the philosophy of sports activities is very compatible with our mission.

skill recieved: #ability to read photos

1,750 hryvnia was collected and the money has already been transferred to the project! We thank everyone who came, learned a little about us, asked around after training. And we thank Elena, her husband Sergey and Alexander Aslanov for this opportunity - to be healthy and help!
