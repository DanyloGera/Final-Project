Finally, we can tell you what important (apart from grocery distribution and strategic planning) our team has been doing for the last three months. For a number of objective reasons, the "Old" social project has become an autonomous legal entity.

In March 2017, a charitable foundation was registered «Старенькі». We received all the necessary documents, the status of non-profitability and switched completely to our own support. Now we are here http://starenki.com.ua/ We went to this decision for a long time, having weighed all the pros and cons, we understood that we needed to take such an important step for development.

This was preceded by a long and eventful journey. The project team began joint volunteer activities at the end of 2015. Initially, we chose for ourselves a strategy of transparency of intentions, therefore we signed an official memorandum of cooperation with МБФ Let’s Help — Давай Допоможемо, to the sub-account of which all the funds collected by us were received until March 2017.That is, we do not accept cash donations from the first day, and all reporting on income and expenses was kept by an accountant МБФ «Давай допоможемо». During this year we have grown a lot, formed a community of people willing to support, worked out a mechanism of assistance,we are recognized, we have plans to move forward, and we understand that the initiative «Старенькі» with us now forever. For this reason and many others, it was decided to register their own charitable foundation.

The path of a social project began «Старенькі». The basic principles that we identified for ourselves at the very beginning of the path and continue to adhere to them now are:

  • No cash receipts or expenses.
  • Full transparency of the project.
  • We do not earn anything and do not represent any of the commercial organizations (or political associations).
  • Our project is a social initiative.
  • We are not promoting anyone, but we always sincerely say "thank you" for your help and participation.
